Seven Letters (Poem) - Lesli

Seven Letters

by Lesli Tabile

How many months have gone by?

Many times have I tried to hide.

Whenever the two pass each other by,

They avoid each other's stare and presence.

Sometimes, I sit here and wonder

If even for an instance

I ever crossed your mind

Out of guilt and contemplation

Unsaid thoughts remain unsaid

And if the other finally gathers the courage

To confront, to apologize, to forgive.

Only will the other be reminded

Of their "friendship"

Remaining as distant friends

Slowly turned into what they were before they met.

I wanted to say that "I'm sorry"

Been waiting for so long

To say these simple, short seven letters

Hoping that it isn't too late...
